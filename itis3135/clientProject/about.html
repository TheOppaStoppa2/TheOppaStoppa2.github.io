About Caroline

Caroline and her family have been proud New York Mets fans for generations. Growing up in a family full of die-hard supporters, she’s attended countless games at Citi Field and has followed the team through every high and low.

Her favorite player is Pete Alonso, known for his incredible home runs and leadership on and off the field. Caroline admires his work ethic, positive energy, and dedication to the team.

Fun facts about the Mets:

  • The team was founded in 1962 as a replacement for the Dodgers and Giants after they moved west.
  • They’ve won two World Series titles — in 1969 and 1986.
  • Their mascot, Mr. Met, is one of the most recognizable in baseball.
