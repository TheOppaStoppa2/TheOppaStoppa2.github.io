Website Evaluations

B-Egg Farm

The B-Egg Farm website is a clean, modern, and visually appealing site that highlights fresh eggs, sustainability, and local farming. The design feels welcoming and easy to navigate.

  • Contrast: The site uses good color contrast with a white background and bold black text, paired with high-quality images that pop.
  • Repetition: Fonts, colors, and button styles are consistent throughout the site, reinforcing brand identity.
  • Alignment: Everything is neatly aligned in grids, making the content easy to read and scan.
  • Proximity: Related content (like product details and images) are grouped together logically, avoiding clutter.

Seiryu-Kan

The Seiryu-Kan website is confusing and outdated. It is difficult to read, has poor structure, and lacks a clear sense of design hierarchy.

  • Contrast: Low contrast colors make the text hard to read against the background.
  • Repetition: Styles and formatting are inconsistent, with different fonts and random design elements across pages.
  • Alignment: Content often looks scattered with no clear grid, making it visually overwhelming.
  • Proximity: Unrelated elements are crammed together, while important information is spread too far apart, creating confusion.
